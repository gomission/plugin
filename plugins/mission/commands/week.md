# /mission week

Two blocks:

1. **This week's paragraph** — generated by the `week-prompt` skill. Same voice rules as Today. Focus: what closes this week + which goals are drifting.
2. **Microgoals moving this week** — one line per microgoal, with per-line affordances:
   - `[Promote to Today]` → user replies with the microgoal ID; it's inserted into Today's next generation as a required inclusion
   - `[Defer to next week]`
   - `[Kill]` → captured as negative training signal per evidence discipline

## Modifiers

- `--edit` — Present last generated Week prompt in editable form.
- `--promote MICROGOAL_ID` — Skip generation. Mark the microgoal as required-for-today and confirm.

## Silent-microgoals rule

If any active microgoal has had zero movement in the last 5 days, flag it explicitly at the end of the second block: "Silent 5+ days: <microgoal>". This is the highest-leverage signal — do not omit.
